AVL Trees

What are they?

AVL trees are another example of a self balancing tree. The rule to keep in mind for these trees is that the difference in height must be
less than or equal to 1 (height<=1).

AVL trees self-correct when this rule is violated, using rotations. In code, this consists of redirecting pointers, which we will see later.
